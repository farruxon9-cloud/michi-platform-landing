The Platform

All Conveniences in One App

Michi consolidates all critical tools and conveniences for drivers in Japan into a single, comprehensive mobile super-app. From job matching to fleet optimization, we bring efficiency directly to the road.

By eliminating recruitment middlemen, we cut costs for fleets while maximizing daily earnings and flexibility for the active workforce.

Job Ads & Arubaito

Instant access to part-time and full-time driver listings across Japan.

Auto Service Booking

Pre-book vehicle inspections (Shaken) and maintenance to eliminate waiting times.

Voice AI Assistant

Drivers command all application features using safe, hands-free conversational dialogue.

Offline Navigation Maps

Offline and online GPS maps optimized for various vehicle sizes and cargo loads.
